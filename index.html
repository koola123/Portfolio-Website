That's Me

I’m experienced in sales and a passionate full-stack web developer.

How have I come to IT?

Before entering the IT world professionally, I worked in sales and as a consultant for IT & Technology at an internationally operating US-staffing company. In that role, I met many IT-specialists, became fascinated by their profession, particularly their extensive knowledge, the way they work and their projects. Working with IT professionals was a great experience and, finally, it was the catalyst to make a fundamental change in my career path from Sales to IT.


Having made that decision, I started searching for the most suitable way to becoming a professional web developer. Pursuing a related academic program was not an option for me due to my full-time work commitments. Finally, I discovered the most feasible solution: an online-bootcamp. I came across CareerFoundry, a reputable Berlin-based company for further education in the field of IT and realized that CareerFoundry offered comprehensive IT-programs. I decided to enroll in their “Full-Stack Web Developer” program, which perfectly aligned with my expectations and aspirations.

My education as a Web Developer

Throughout the program I gained extensive knowledge in modern web technologies such as React, React Native, Angular, and other state-of-the-art JavaScript libraries and frameworks. Additionally, I delved into serverless functions in the cloud, including platforms like Amazon Web Services. Needless to say, completing the program was a tough and challenging endeavor, but it was also an incredibly exciting experience for me. On May 30th, 2023, I successfully completed that program having worked on six bootcamp projects and an additional project that I developed based on a boiler-plate-code tutorial from YouTube. For a first impression, please take a look at my GitHub where I have deployed all of my self-coded projects.

My expectations as a Web Developer

As a trained web developer, I am keen to make a valuable contribution to the projects I'm involved in and, thereby, deepen my knowledge in particular in the field of Full-Stack or Frontend Development. I'm also looking for inspiring networks, interesting and open-minded people, as well as a work environment which offers me opportunities to grow in the job and is giving me the chance to take more responsibilities over time. Hence, Freelancing is not an option to me at this point of time.

Following my passion as a dedicated traveller I am also open to new opportunities, including new cultural spaces, and a flexible approach to my professional life, whether it be in Germany, Europe or beyond.
